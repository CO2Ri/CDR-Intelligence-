The inversion: The regions with the highest CDR potential — tropical soils for ERW, coastal oceans for mCDR, vast land for BiCRS, abundant solar for DAC — received almost nothing. India attracted $3M total before 2024. Sub-Saharan Africa received under $15M across seven years. The geography of CDR investment and the geography of CDR need are almost perfectly inverted.
